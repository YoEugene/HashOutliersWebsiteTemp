Aaron Chuang

Business Development Director

Aaron is the explorer of #Outliers
and an outlaw in the startup field.

Having started investing in the stock market since 19 years old, Aaron established his own asset management company at the age of 25, building his team from 2 employees to more than 40. He sold the company three years later after having experienced all the ups and downs, made every possible mistake that an entrepreneur could make, and acquiring two companies. Aaron’s career then progressed to working in financial institutions that specialized in project financing, buy-out leveraging, and financial valuation models corroboration, mandating international syndication loan (The Bank of East Asia) across APAC (i.e. China, Korea, Hong Kong, Singapore, and Taiwan).

In addition to Aaron’s interests in Economics, he was also a cram school teacher where he taught Chinese literature and history. He has also been a Go coach for more than five years.

To Aaron, “Outliers” are forerunners who pioneer the adventure.
