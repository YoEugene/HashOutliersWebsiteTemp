Peggy Pan

Program Director

Peggy is the fuel of
#Outliers and a maven in storytelling.

Having advised over 30 brands, ranging from MNCs, startups to governments, Peggy holds unique and extensive experiences in strategizing brand positioning, developing go-to-market approaches and executing creative campaigns at both national and global scales. Peggy began her career as a Graduate Fellow at Ogilvy, where she gained multidisciplinary training and insights. In search for new defining elements in branding and creativity, Peggy later joined the KYU collective and established RedPeak’s first office in Asia.

Peggy is committed to driving teams to success and has taken leadership roles in global youth organizations, such as AIESEC, Global Shapers Community and Jumpstart. With a great sense of humor and a deep love for humanity, Peggy is also an engaging presenter/moderator and a jolly person all around. Peggy graduated from National Chiao Tung University (Taiwan) and is currently obtaining her MBA degree at Stanford University.

To Peggy, “Outliers” are those who launch moonshots for a better world.
